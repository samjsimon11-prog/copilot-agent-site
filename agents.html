Agents & Roles

Each agent has a clear tone and responsibility.

Customer Service Agent

Frontline Support
Tone: friendly, empathetic Job: clarify user needs, detect issues

This agent listens carefully, asks clarifying questions, and spots problems early. It’s built to make users feel understood while surfacing what’s really going wrong.

Research & Analysis Agent

Facts & Summaries
Tone: analytical, structured Job: extract facts, summarize, verify

This agent digs through information, pulls out the key points, and checks for consistency. It’s your go‑to for clean summaries and grounded research.

Business Strategy Agent

Operations & Feasibility
Tone: executive, decisive Job: evaluate feasibility, risk, operations

This agent thinks like a strategist—balancing risk, resources, and execution. It helps turn ideas into realistic plans that could actually work.

Financial & Market Analysis Agent

Numbers & ROI
Tone: quantitative, skeptical Job: evaluate numbers, markets, ROI

This agent challenges assumptions with data. It looks at costs, returns, and market signals to keep decisions grounded in reality—not just vibes.

Unlimited‑Input Agent

Long Text Handling
Tone: neutral Job: handle long text, chunking, merging

This agent is built for huge inputs—documents, transcripts, long chats. It breaks them into chunks, processes them, and stitches everything back together coherently.

Sales & Persuasion Agent

Messaging & Pitch
Tone: energetic, persuasive Job: evaluate messaging, market fit

This agent focuses on how things sound—headlines, pitches, emails, landing pages. It tunes language for clarity, emotion, and conversion.

Industry‑Specific Agent

Domain Expertise
Tone: expert Job: apply domain knowledge

This agent brings specialized knowledge to the table. It understands the jargon, constraints, and patterns of a specific industry and uses that to refine answers.

Master Strategy Agent (CEO AI)

Synthesis & Direction
Tone: authoritative, synthesizing Job: combine all insights into a unified strategy

This agent sits at the top. It listens to all the other agents, merges their insights, and proposes a clear direction—like a CEO making the final call.
