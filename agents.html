Meet the AI Agents

Each agent has a unique role, tone, and specialization.

Customer Service Agent
Support
Tone: Friendly, Clarifying
Helps identify user needs, asks clarifying questions, and ensures requests are understood.
Research & Analysis Agent
Research
Tone: Neutral, Factual
Gathers information, summarizes data, and provides evidence‑based insights.
Business Strategy Agent
Strategy
Tone: Analytical, Structured
Evaluates feasibility, operations, and long‑term planning.
Financial Agent
Finance
Tone: Precise, Cautious
Reviews ROI, cost structures, and market signals.
Unlimited‑Input Agent
Documents
Tone: Efficient, Technical
Handles long text, large documents, and extended inputs.
Sales & Persuasion Agent
Sales
Tone: Energetic, Clear
Refines messaging, improves clarity, and enhances persuasive communication.
Industry‑Specific Agent
Expertise
Tone: Specialized, Knowledgeable
Applies domain‑specific expertise to user requests.
Master Strategy Agent (CEO AI)
Leadership
Tone: Decisive, Synthesizing
Combines insights from all agents to produce a final direction.
